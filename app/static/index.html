Session cookies (optional — fixes LinkedIn & Instagram)

LinkedIn and Instagram login-wall anonymous requests almost completely (confirmed: LinkedIn returns a bot-block, Instagram rate-limits/redirects to login). Paste a cookie from your own logged-in browser session and these platforms are fetched authenticated as you — the same mechanism a real signed-in browser tab uses.
From your browser: DevTools → Application/Storage → Cookies → linkedin.com → li_at.
DevTools → Cookies → instagram.com → sessionid.

These are your own account's cookies, used only to make requests as you — the same as staying signed in in a browser tab. Treat them like passwords: anyone with the cookie can act as your session. Stored server-side the same way as the AI key.
